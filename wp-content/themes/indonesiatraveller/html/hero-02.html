CIFOR-ICRAF is a centre of science and development excellence that harnesses the benefits of trees for people and the environment.

Born of the merger of the Center for International Forestry Research (CIFOR) and World Agroforestry (ICRAF), the centre works closely with the CIFOR-ICRAF Group – comprised of the Global Landscape Forum, the CGIAR Research Program on Forests, Trees and Agroforestry, and Resilient Landscapes – to strengthen the connection between science and development.
